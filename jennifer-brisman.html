VOW by Jennifer Brisman

Guest management app

User Interface

Project Overview : The purpose of this project was to design a mobile application that provides meaningful and relevant experiences for users to manage their live events.

Based on the user research, I developed concepts and wireframes to outline the app's structure and key features. I collaborated with the development team and stakeholders to ensure the feasibility and alignment of the designs with the app's goals. In terms of visual design, I leveraged my expertise in UI design to create visually appealing and on-brand designs for the app. I took into consideration the app's usability, user flow, and the overall user experience while designing various screens, buttons, icons, and other visual elements.
