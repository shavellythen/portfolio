Ever Brighter

Campaign for the University of Miami

UX / UI Design

Project Overview : The objective of this project was to design a visually appealing, user-friendly, and engaging website for the University of Miami's 2023 campaign "Ever Brighter."

The website serves as a central platform to promote the campaign's goals, raise awareness, and encourage donations from various stakeholders, including alumni, faculty, staff, and the general public. The design focuses on creating a positive and impactful user experience (UX) and a visually pleasing user interface (UI) to effectively convey the campaign's message and encourage visitors to take action.

Deliverables:
Wireframes
Visual Design
Interactive Prototypes
Design Assets
Design Documentation

Conclusion: The university philanthropical campaign website design project aims to create a compelling, user-centric digital experience that effectively communicates the campaign's message and motivates visitors to contribute to the cause. By prioritizing visual appeal, intuitive navigation, and seamless user interactions, the website plays a pivotal role in generating awareness, fostering donor engagement, and achieving the campaign's goals.
